About Us

Discover the story behind Cozy Cup Café.


Cozy Cup Cafe emerged from a passion for creating a warm and inviting space for coffee lovers to gather, relax, and enjoy exceptional brews. Established in 2023, our café has become a cherished part of the community. Our founders, John Wick, envisioned a place that goes beyond being just a coffee shop – a haven where people can escape the bustle of daily life and savor the simple joys of a well-crafted cup of coffee.


Quality is the cornerstone of our mission. From the moment coffee beans are carefully selected to the precise brewing methods we employ, we are dedicated to delivering an unparalleled coffee experience. CozyCup is committed to providing the highest quality beverages, prepared with passion and served with a smile.


Join us on this journey where every cup tells a story, and every guest is a cherished part of the CozyCup community.
